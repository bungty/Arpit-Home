The Arpit Home Legacy

Founded by visionary entrepreneur Mr. Vikram Singhania, Arpit Home began as a dream to create a comfortable hospitality experience that would bridge traditional Indian warmth with modern service standards. What started as a single property in Gurugram has evolved into a symbol of reliable comfort in the heart of India's corporate capital.

Inspired by the need for quality accommodation in Gurugram's growing business district, our founders envisioned a space where every guest would feel welcomed and valued. From the practical room layouts to the modern amenities, every detail was meticulously planned to create a satisfying experience.

Over the years, we have proudly hosted business travelers, families, and visitors from across India, each adding to our rich tapestry of stories and experiences. Our commitment to value and service has earned us consistent positive reviews, but our greatest achievement remains the satisfaction of our guests.
