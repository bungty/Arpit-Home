Couples are welcome at Arpit Home. We ensure a comfortable and respectful environment for all our guests.

Check-in Requirements

Guests can check in using any local or outstation ID proof (PAN card not accepted). Valid government-issued photo ID is mandatory for registration.

Please note that services like early check-in, extra guests, and heaters in cold areas are available at additional charges. These services can be arranged upon request at the reception.
